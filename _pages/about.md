I am **Heng Wei (魏珩)**, a Ph.D. student at Nanjing Forestry University. My research focuses on **global change and ecosystem carbon sinks**, with particular attention to carbon sink dynamics in croplands and karst ecosystems.

My work combines ecological process understanding, climate-change analysis, and spatial data to evaluate how ecosystems respond to environmental change and how their carbon sink functions can be better understood.

## Research Interests

- Global change and ecosystem carbon sinks
- Cropland carbon source-sink dynamics
- Karst vegetation carbon sinks
- Karst soil conservation
- Climate impacts on terrestrial ecosystems

## Publications

My published work covers cropland carbon sinks, karst vegetation carbon sink change, phosphorus limitation in karst regions, and soil conservation evaluation. See the full list on the [Publications page](/publications/).
